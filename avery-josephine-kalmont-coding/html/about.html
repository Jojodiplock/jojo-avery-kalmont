Our Purpose

Kamlot is dedicated to educating outdoor adventurers, tourists, and local residents on winter wilderness safety. We provide essential information on avalanche preparedness, response strategies, hypothermia prevention, and first aid. With real-time weather and avalanche risk updates, we aim to keep you informed and safe as you enjoy the mountains.
