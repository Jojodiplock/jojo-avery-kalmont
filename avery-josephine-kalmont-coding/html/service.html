No matter how you explore the backcountry, the snow and weather conditions that cause avalanches remain the same. However, different groups experience and navigate the mountains in unique ways. Snowmobilers cover more ground at higher speeds than skiers, ice climbers spend extended time in avalanche-prone areas, and snowshoers typically stick to gentler terrain.

Search and Rescue

The Kalmont Search and Rescue Team is a dedicated group of highly trained volunteers and professionals committed to saving lives in the rugged and unpredictable terrain of Kalmont. Whether responding to lost hikers, injured climbers, or avalanche emergencies, the team operates around the clock, braving harsh weather and challenging landscapes to bring people to safety.

Equipped with specialized gear, local expertise, and unwavering determination, Kalmonts rescue team is the lifeline for adventurers in distress. Their mission is simple: to protect, assist, and rescue those who find themselves in danger on the slopes of Kalmont.
